Survival Of The Fittest pits your wits and nervest against the "Laws of Nature" and against your opponent's colonies in a battle for survival and dominance.  You must create colonies of organisms that will survive and grow in the universe of computer life, and attack and defend against  your opponent's life forms.  You will find Survival Of The Fittest to be a fascinating and challenging test of strategic and tactical skill.  This action game cartridge for one or two players has three games, earch with six different play options.
