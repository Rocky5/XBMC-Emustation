Penguin Wars
Platform: Nintendo Game Boy
Region: USA
Media: Cartridge
Controller: Gameboy
Genre: Action
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 1990
Developer: ASCII Entertainment

A penguin rolls spheres across a table attempting to knock its opponent out for points. The character with the fewest spheres on its side of the table at the end of the timed match is the winner.
