Control Puss in Boots and his friends, Shrek and Donkey, as you experience the Shrek 2 movie from an all new perspective. Fight off all new enemies using an extensive set of acrobatic maneuvers and combo attacks, collect gold coins to unlock hidden bonus features, and play as the hit new character from the Shrek series in more than twenty challenging levels.
