Thunderbirds: International Rescue is a game based on a British television series form mid-1960.

Jeff Tracy, assisted by his five sons, directs the activities of International Rescue an organization who uses technically advanced vehicles called Thunderbirds in death-defying escapades around the world in order to save those in need. You take control of one of the 8 possible vehicles to complete the missions you are assigned. Missions include destroying enemy radars, rescuing civilians etc.
