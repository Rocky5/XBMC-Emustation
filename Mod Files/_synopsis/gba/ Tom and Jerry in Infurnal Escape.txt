Tom and Jerry in Infurnal Escape
Platform: Nintendo Game Boy Advance
Region: USA
Media: Cartridge
Controller: Gameboy Advance
Genre: Platformer
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 2003

After getting flattened by a grand piano, Tom finds himself in the underworld, boiling in a cauldron of spike's cat stew. In order to help Tom restore his good karma and return to his normal life, you must successfully perform missions that have been outlined by Toodles the Angel. If not, the pitchfork awaits!
