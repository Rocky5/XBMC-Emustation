Genre: Platformer
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 2002
Developer: Planet Interactive

_________________________
An adventure from a distant age brought to the 21st century by the wonders of genetic science! Kong is back, but his magical island is under attack from an evil scientist determined to destroy all in the name of power. Take control of the biologically engineered Kong and his childhood friend Jason and combat a host of terrifying enemies to protect the Primal Stones, or the world as you know it will cease to exist.
