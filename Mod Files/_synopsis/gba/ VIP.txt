V.I.P.
Platform: Nintendo Game Boy Advance
Region: Europe
Media: Cartridge
Controller: Gameboy Advance
Genre: Action
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 2002
Developer: Planet Interactive
Publisher: Ubisoft
_________________________
VIP is based on the TV Series of the same name. You play as the 5 members of the VIP Team, including Pamela Anderson as Vallery Irons.

The game is a side-scroller with 8 levels. In this game, you are loaded with firepower, and you'll cruise the seedy Los Angeles underworld on crime-bustin' missions. And when L.A.'s most notorious mobster kidnaps an elite plastic surgeon, it's up to you to spearhead the rescue!
