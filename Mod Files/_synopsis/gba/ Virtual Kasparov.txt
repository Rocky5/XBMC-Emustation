Virtual Kasparov
Platform: Nintendo Game Boy Advance
Region: USA
Media: Cartridge
Controller: Gameboy Advance
Genre: Board Game
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 2002
Developer: Titus Software
Publisher: Titus Software
_________________________
Virtual Kasparov is a chess game with thirty-one opponents of varying difficulty to face in a story mode. Victory over this multi-cultural group of chess players will lead you to Gary Kasparov himself.

Various styles of boards are included along with several 3D boards. Multiplayer is supported via link-cable or on a single system.
