Cool Bricks
Platform: Nintendo Game Boy Color
Region: Europe
Media: Cartridge
Controller: Gameboy Color
Genre: Puzzle
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 1999
Developer: Freestylez
Publisher: SCi
_________________________
Cool Bricks is a Breakout-style game, updated for the modern market. Players use the bat and ball to smash all the breakable bricks to complete each level. Cool Bricks also features a wide range of power-ups including the Ghost, Ninja and Poison. New technological tricks allow for high-colour screens and in excess of 150 levels. The game also features a durable High-Score board.
